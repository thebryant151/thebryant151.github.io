Beef Dendeng

Serve: 6 Person

Cook Time: 61 Minutes

-Love to Cook, Love to Eat, and Love to Live

the best and most authentic beef rendang recipe you will find online! Spicy, rich and creamy Malaysian/Indonesian beef stew made with beef, spices and coconut milk.

Ingredient

500g Beef 20g Ginger
1 Stalk Lemongrass 2 L Water
40 g Corn Oil 40 g Corn Oil
200 g Onion 40 g Garlic
20 g Ginger 3 g Coriander Powder
50 g Dried Chilli 45 g Water
30 g Sweet Soy Sauce 15 g Thick Soya Sauce
12 g MAGGI® CukupRasa™ 24 g Tamarind Paste
MAGGI® CukupRasa™

How To Cook

Step 1

In a pot, heat up the water with ginger & lemongrass. Add the beef and boil together until tender.


Step 2

After it is cooked, set aside the beef and slice into 1 cm thick pieces. Using a pestle & mortar, pound the meat into flat pieces. Set aside.


Step 3

Heat oil, fry meat until slightly browned. Set aside on kitchen towels..


Step 4

Stir fry blended ingredients until oil separated. Then add water, sweet soy sauce, dark soy sauce, MAGGI® Cukup Rasa™ and tamarind juice. Stir until the sauce thickens.

Step 5

Add in the beef and mix it well or until the gravy coats the meat.

Step 6

Serve hot.
