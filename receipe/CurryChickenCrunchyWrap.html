Curry Chicken Crunchy Wrap

Serve:  1 Person

Cook Time: 41 Minute

-Love to Cook, Love to Eat, and Love to Live

Tasty lunch for busy you? This crunchy wrap is tasty, healthy, easy-to-make, and easy-to-carry!

Ingredient

300g Chicken Breast 1 Onion
1 tbsp Curry Powder 1 Tomato
0.25 cup Greek Yogurt 2 sprigs Coriander leaf
4 tbsp Cooking Oil 1 Cucumber
5 pcs Tortilla Wraps 5 leaves Salad
2 tbsp Lemon Juice 1 cup NESTLÉ® GOLD™ Cornflakes
1.5 tbsp MAGGI® CukupRasa™ 30g MAGGI® Sambal Tumis
5 tbsp MAGGI® Chilli Sauce

How To Cook

Step 1

Mix well curry powder, MAGGI® Sambal Tumis, MAGGI® Cukup Rasa and greek yogurt. Coat the chicken and let it marinate for 30 min.


Step 2

Coat the chicken with crushed NESTLÉ® GOLD™ Cornflakes. Heat cooking oil, fry until crisp. Set it aside.


Step 3

Mix sliced onion, sliced tomato, sliced coriander leaf, MAGGI® Cukup Rasa and lemon juice until well combined.


Step 4

Arrange salad leaves, tomato slices and cucumber on tortilla bread. Place the fried chicken. Place the salsa and wrap neatly.


Step 5

Grill on a flat pan. Ready to enjoy with MAGGI® Chili Sauce.
