Chicken Burritos

Serve:  5 Person

Cook Time: 15 Minute

-Love to Cook, Love to Eat, and Love to Live

Burritos are a popular Tex-Mex dish across the United States. It is a stuffed tortilla roll filled with a variety of meats, cheeses and vegetables — give it an extra kick of flavour by topping it with sauces, salsas or sour cream!

Ingredient

2 Chicken Fillet 50g Grated Mozzarella Cheese
0.5 cup Self-Rising Flour 20g Mixed Salad
0.5 cup Water 6 pcs Tortilla Wraps
1tbsp Mayonnaise 0.5 tbsp MAGGI® CukupRasa™
1 tbsp MAGGI® Tomato Ketchup 1 tbsp MAGGI® Chilli Sauce

How To Cook

Step 1

Slice chicken fillets into long slices.


Step 2

Make a batter by stirring in flour and ½ cup water and add in MAGGI® CukupRasa™.


Step 3

Dip chicken slices into batter and shallow fry for 5-8 minutes.


Step 4

Top tortilla with chicken slices, salad, cheese and tangy sauce before rolling it into a cylinder.


Step 5

Repeat with remaining tortillas and enjoy.
