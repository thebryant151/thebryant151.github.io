Inchi Kabin

Serve: 2 Person

Cook Time: 22 Minute

-Love to Cook, Love to Eat, and Love to Live

Here’s your next go-to recipe for juicy fried chicken at its best!

Ingredient

500g Chicken Breast 1 tbsp Light Soy Sauce
50g Cooking Oil 5 Stalks Lemongrass
2 tbsp Honey 3 cloves Garlic
1 tbsp Corn Oil 20g Ginger
1 tbsp Sesame Oil 100g Cooking Oil
3 tbsp MAGGI® Oyster Sauce 1 tbsp MAGGI® Concentrated Chicken Stock

How To Cook

Step 1

The night before cooking, blend the lemongrass, garlic, ginger, shallots and curry leaves together.


Step 2

When the ingredients have formed into a fine paste, marinate the chicken pieces with it, along with the chilli powder, cumin powder, coriander powder, aniseed powder, MAGGI® Oyster Sauce, MAGGI® Concentrated Chicken Stock, honey, sesame oil and light soy sauce.


Step 3

Leave it to marinate overnight.


Step 4

When you’re about to cook the chicken, heat up the oil in a wok. Then, fry the marinated chicken until it turns a crisp golden brown.


Step 5

Do serve it while it’s hot. This simple, but oh-so-mouth-watering recipe will become a sure-fire hit with your family.
