Family Favourite Beef Stew

Serve: 6 Person

Cook Time: 45 Minutes

-Love to Cook, Love to Eat, and Love to Live

Stew is a dish of poultry or seafood cooked together with vegetables and other ingredients in liquid in a closed vessel over low heat, for a hearty soup that’ll warm your stomach and heart. It is a cooking process that tenderizes tougher foods and mingles flavours, commonly eaten in cold countries with bread. Now considered a comfort food, stews were once a meal of necessity for communities who had little to eat.

Ingredient

1 tbsp Corn Oil 350 g Beef
1 tbs Flour 1 Onion
10 Cloves Garlic 1 tsp Oregano
1 tsp Thyme 2 pcs Bay Leaf
2 tsp Paprika 2.5 cups Water
1 tbsp Tomato Paste 0.25 cup MAGGI® Tomato Ketchup
0.5 Potatoes 0.25 Green Capsicum
0.25 Red Capsicum 0.5 Leek
1 Small Carrot 1 steam Celery
2 cubes MAGGI® Chicken Stock Cube MAGGI® Chicken Stock Cube

How To Cook

Step 1

Heat pan with oil, in a bowl mix beef and flour. Panfry beef until half cooked or golden brown. Set aside.


Step 2

Using the same wok from beef, stir fry onion, garlic, oregano, bay leaf, paprika until aromatic and add in beef. Pour in water, tomato paste and MAGGI Tomato Sauce. Stir thoroughly.


Step 3

After boiling, add in ingredients potato, capsicum, leek, carrot, celery and simmer for 15 minutes.


Step 4

Add in MAGGI CHICKEN CUBE, stir well and simmer another 10 minutes with low heat. Let the gravy become slightly thick.
