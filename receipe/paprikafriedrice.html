Paprika Fried Rice

Serve: 6 Person

Cook Time: 15 Minutes

-Love to Cook, Love to Eat, and Love to Live

Quick, easy and wonderfully flavoursome, this fried rice from the north brings in some heat! Following a extremly simple 3 step cooking process, you will be able to make a delicous bowl of fried rice that packs a punch for your families and friends.

Ingredient

3 tbsp Cooking Oil 4 cloves Garlic
2 Red Onion 100 g Chicken Breast
1 tbsp Paprika Powder 100 g Green Capsicum
100 g Red Capsicum 500 g Cooked Rice
1 tbsp Fried Shallots 1 tsp MAGGI CukupRasa

How To Cook

Step 1

Sauté garlic and red onion until fragrant.


Step 2

Add in chicken, paprika powder, green capsicum, red capsicum, rice and MAGGI® CukupRasa™. Stir thoroughly.


Step 3

Serve while hot.
