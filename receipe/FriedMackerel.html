Fried Mackerel

Serve: 6 Person

Cook Time: 16 Minutes

-Love to Cook, Love to Eat, and Love to Live

Make the most out of this simple recipe that requires minimal ingredients. Simply delicious, this great family favourite is best served with steamed rice

Ingredient

3 Mackerel Fish 1 tsp Turmeric Powder
0.25 cup oil 1.5 tbsp MAGGI CukupRasa

How To Cook

Step 1

Firstly, place the fish in a big bowl. Next, add the turmeric and MAGGI® CukupRasa™ to the fish. Rub the mixture onto the fish and also inside the stomach cavity. Let it marinate for a few minutes while you get the wok ready.


Step 2

Then, heat enough oil to deep fry. Gently add the fish to the wok. Fry the fish until it turns golden brown on both sides.


Step 3

Lastly, remove the fish from the wok and set it on a serving plate.
