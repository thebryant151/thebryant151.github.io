Seafood Fried Rice

Serve: 2 Person

Cook Time: 10 Minutes

-Love to Cook, Love to Eat, and Love to Live

Fried rice is a dish for amateur home cooks to learn and professional chefs to master. For home cooks, fried rice is a fuss-free dish, just toss in any leftover rice from yesterday's dinner and extra ingredients and fry it all up for a filling meal. For professional chefs in fancy restaurants and banquet halls, the dish is a skill test to impart skills to achieve the right consistency in taste and texture. One of the most important tips to make your fried rice taste good: prepare the rice beforehand. You only want to use thoroughly chilled-cooked rice because freshly cooked rice will turn into soggy clumps as it hits the wok's high temperature. Being the highly versatile dish that it is, feel free to swap the ingredients for anything you prefer or have in your fridge! Need a quick yet filling dish for your family? This seafood fried rice dish is a simple and filling comfort food that can be prepared easily within a short time!

Ingredient

4 cups Rice Cooked 2 tbsp Corn Oil
2 Shallots 3 cloves Garlic
6 Bird's Eye Chilli 1 tbsp Anchovies
8 prawns 2 pcs Squid
2 smalls Carrot 12 pcs Snow Peas
3 pcs Crab Stick 2 pcs Oyster Mushrooms
1 tbsp MAGGI CukupRasa

How To Cook

Step 1

In a wok, stir fry shallots, garlic, bird's eye chilli and anchovies until fragrant.


Step 2

Add in prawns, squid, carrot, sweet peas, crab stick, king oyster mushroom and MAGGIÂ® CukupRasa. Stir well until cooked.


Step 3

In a wok, pour in the rice and stir well.


Step 4

Once cooked, serve in a plate while still hot.
