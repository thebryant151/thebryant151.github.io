Stir Fried Maggi Tom Yam

Serve: 2 Person

Cook Time: 8 Minutes

-Love to Cook, Love to Eat, and Love to Live

A combination of sour, spicy and savoury flavours — tom yam is a distinctively Thai dish. Tom yam is viewed as a crucial element of Thai heritage because it represents the local agricultural communities, reflecting the local dietary culture that has remained constant over time. The tom yam recipe is mainly presented in two versions: a clear soup broth or a dry tom yam that is stir-fried to perfection. The traditional tom yam broth is a clear soup which becomes slightly richer in taste cooked with fat from prawns’ heads. The dry version uses the same herbs and seasonings from the soup, retaining the tom yam recipe’s same tasty flavour — minus the broth! Some examples are tom yam flavoured sauce in a stir-fried seafood dish, fried tom yam noodles and tom yam fried rice.

Ingredient

2 packages MAGGI® 2-Minute Noodles Tom Yam 1 tbsp Corn Oil
2 pcs Squid 5 Mediums Prawns
1 pc Kaffir lime leaves 3 Bird's Eye CHilli
5 tbsp Water 1 Small Carrot
2 smalls Carrot 12 pcs Snow Peas
50 g Chinese Cabbage 50 g Cauliflower
1 tbsp MAGGI CukupRasa

How To Cook

Step 1

Blanch MAGGI® 2-Minute Noodles Tom Yam without seasoning for 1 minute and toss.


Step 2

In a wok, stir fry squid, prawns, Kaffir lime leaves, bird's eye chilli, MAGGI® 2-Minute Noodles Tom Yam seasoning and water until evenly mixed and fragrant.


Step 3

Add in carrots, Chineese cabbage and cauliflower. Stir for 1 minute.


Step 4

Add in the cooked noodles. Stir for 1 minute or until evenly mixed.
