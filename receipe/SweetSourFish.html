Sweet Sour Fish

Serve: 6 Person

Cook Time: 26 Minutes

-Love to Cook, Love to Eat, and Love to Live

Have you ever wondered why fish dishes are mostly cooked and served whole (head and tail intact) in Chinese restaurants? It is a symbolic significance of good luck and abundance. These days, you can find an abundance of steamed and fried fish recipes online to enjoy restaurant-style fish in the comfort of your own home! No matter what your cooking method is, be it steaming the fish to retain the silky, smooth texture of the meat or fry it for a light, crunchy outer skin; the sauce is one of the most important elements to make a fish taste good.

Ingredient

1 Seabass 2 tbsp Cornstarch
100g Corn Oil 1 Holland Onions
3 Cloves Garlic 1 in Ginger
3 Bird's Eye Chilli 0.25 Capsicum, red, raw
0.25cup MAGGI® Tomato Ketchup 0.25cup MAGGI® Chilli Sauce
5g Coriander 1 tbsp MAGGI CukupRasa

How To Cook

Step 1

Clean the fish, coat with MAGGI® CukupRasa and cornstarch. Heat the oil and fry until golden and cooked. Set aside.


Step 2

With about 2 tablespoon of oil, sauce the yellow onion, garlic and ginger until fragrant.


Step 3

Add in bird's Eye Chili, capsicum, tomato and pineapple. Fry until well combine.


Step 4

Add in MAGGI Chili Sauce and MAGGI Tomato Sauce. Mix well. Add in water and let it simmer.


Step 5

Pour sauce over fish, garnish with coriander leaves. and ready to serve.
