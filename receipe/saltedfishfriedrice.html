Salted Fish Fried Rice

Serve: 4 Person

Cook Time: 30 Minutes

-Love to Cook, Love to Eat, and Love to Live

This style of fried rice tucked with crispy salted fish will tantalise your taste buds and make your drool for more. Salty and delicious perfect for your friends and family. The best of it, it really simple and easy to make. By following the simple 4 steps recipe below, you will be able to make delicious salted fish fried rice for your friends and family members.

Ingredient

3 cloves Garlic 2 tbsp Corn Oil
2 tsp Salted Fish 1 slice Chicken Breast
4 Bird's Eye Chilli 2.5 cups Rice (cooked)
0.5 packet Kailan 0.5 Cucumber
2 smalls Carrot 12 pcs Snow Peas
1 Chili 1 tbsp MAGGI CukupRasa

How To Cook

Step 1

Heat up wok and pour in corn oil, stir fry garlic, salted fish and bird’s eye chillies.


Step 2

Add chicken, stir fry for 2 minutes.


Step 3

Add rice, kailan and carrots, mix well.


Step 4

Sprinkle MAGGI® CukupRasa, stir for 1 minute and serve.
