Northern Beef Rice Noodle Soup

Serve: 12 Person

Cook Time: 30 Minutes

-Love to Cook, Love to Eat, and Love to Live

Traditionally a northern Vietnamese breakfast specialty, pho bo is now eaten through-out the country at any time of day. Charring the meat, ginger, and shallots gives the broth its complexity. Beef oxtail, although bony and tough, is very flavorful, so it's good for making broth. Partially freeze eye-of-round roast to make it easier to slice. You can also use regular sweet basil in place of Thai basil. It is a hard dish to recreate but by following this simple recipe guide. You will be able to make a soulful delicous beef noodle soup for your friends and family.

Ingredient

400 g Rice Noodles, Dry 3000 g Water, Tap, Drinking
125 g Onions, Raw 2 g Spices, Anise Seed
2 g Spices, Cinnamon, Ground 300 g Beef, lean
200 g Beef, neck bones, cooked 75 g Oil, Corn And Canola
250 g Onions, Raw 18 g Garlic, raw
16 g Ginger Root, Raw 50 g Spices, Allspice, Ground
4 g Lemon Grass (Citronella), Raw 20 g Lime, Musk
40 g Onions, Spring Or Scallions (Includes Tops And Bulb), Raw 100 g Beef, Retail Cuts, Separable Fat, Raw
40 g Tofu, Fried 20 g Mung bean/ Green bean sprouts
100 g Sambal Paste 45g MAGGI CukupRasa

How To Cook

Step 1

In soup pot, add water, onion, star anise, cinnamon, beef, beef bones and corn oil. Let it boil with medium heat until the beef is thoroughly cook.


Step 2

Take out the lean beef, and set aside to cool. (slice beef to serve with noodle)


Step 3

In a pan, heat oil and add in onion, garlic, ginger, soup spices and lemongrass. Stir fry until oil is separated.


Step 4

Add in the paste into the beef stock, stir and let it boil.


Step 5

Boil broth until well combine and season with MAGGI® CukupRasa™.


Step 6

Serve beehoon with hot soup and condiments.
