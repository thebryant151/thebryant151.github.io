Steam Grouper Fish

Serve: 8 Person

Cook Time: 25 Minutes

-Love to Cook, Love to Eat, and Love to Live

Fish that is steamed to perfection, accompanied by a delightfully healthy soup.

Ingredient

1.2 kg Grouper Fish 50g Oil
2 Spring Onion 2L Water
20g Ginger 100g Carrot
150 Tofu Pok 100g Yam
100g Chinese Cabbage 60 ml Milk
2 spring Coriander Leaves 4 MAGGI Ikan Bilis Stock Cube

How To Cook

Step 1

Preheat cubie oven, select HEALTHY STEAM mode press SET adjust temperature to 150˚C press START.


Step 2

In a wok, heat oil and fried fish for 1 – 2 minutes and set aside.


Step 3

After preheating the oven will beep 3 times and the icon “√” appears to infrom the preheat is done.


Step 4

Put the fish in a pot or plate, sprinkle with spring onion and place in cubie oven lower shelf position and set 10 minutes press START.


Step 5

In a pot, boil water, ginger and MAGGI® Ikan Bilis Cube. Blanch carrot, tofu pok, yam, Chinese cabbage, black fungus, tomato and milk in the prepared broth and set aside.


Step 6

Take the fish out and add in the broth. Cooked for 5 minutes with blanched vegetables.


Step 7

Serve while hot.
