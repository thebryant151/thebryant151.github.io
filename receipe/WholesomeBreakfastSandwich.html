Wholesome Breakfast Sandwich

Serve: 2 Person

Cook Time: 10 Minutes

-Love to Cook, Love to Eat, and Love to Live

A hearty and fulfilling breakfast may be the key for you to start a great day. To keep yourself energised and focused, the right meal plate can be just something you need, so doing work won't be such a tough challenge to face. With a cup of hot coffee or tea, a delicious yet simple plate of breakfast such as a toast or a breakfast sandwich can be just the suitable accompaniment for you. Bread has been part and parcel of breakfast in so many regions. Many households keep a stock of sandwich bread in the pantry just in case they need to whip up a quick meal in the morning.

Ingredient

4 slice Wholemeal Bread 2 Egg
4 slice Beef Bacon 1 Onion
1 Green Pepper 1 tbsp MAGGI® Chilli Sauce
1 tsp MAGGI® CukupRasa™ 2 slice Cheese
1 tbsp Oil 15g MAGGI® Chilli Sauce
MAGGI® Chilli Sauce

How To Cook

Step 1

Fry half the amount of onions, green peppers and beef bacon until a little crispy.


Step 2

Add MAGGI® CukupRasa™ and chilli sauce to the eggs and beat well. Pour half of it in the pan and allow it to crisp.


Step 3

Place one slice of bread on top of the egg mixture and let it stick.


Step 4

Flip over, add some chilli sauce and one slice of cheese and cover it with another slice of bread. Flip it over to toast the other side.

Step 5

Repeat steps to prepare another sandwich.
