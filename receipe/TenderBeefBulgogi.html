Tender Beef Bulgogi

Serve: 6 Person

Cook Time: 47 Minutes

Ingredient

500g Beef 1 Pear
5 Garlic 3 Cloves Garlic
3 tbsp Ginger 2 Onion
100 g Shiitake Mushroom 0.25cup Maggi Oyster Sauce
2 tsp Black Pepper 0.5 cup Shiitake Water
2 tbsp Corn Oil tbsp MAGGI CukupRasa

How To Cook

Step 1

In a bowl, comebine beef with the blended pear, garlic, ginger, onion, shiitake mushroom, MAGGIÂ® Oyster Sauce, black pepper and shiitake water.


Step 2

Let the meat marinate overnight or for about 20-30 minutes before cooking.


Step 3

After the meat is well marinated, heat oil in a large skillet and stir fry ingredients until the meat begins to brown, is cooked through and the gravy reduces.


Step 4

When ready, serve Bulgogi with steamed rice and salad on the side.
