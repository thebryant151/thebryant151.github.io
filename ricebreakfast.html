Seafood Fried Rice

Fried rice is a dish for amateur home cooks to learn and professional chefs to master. For home cooks, fried rice is a fuss-free dish, just toss in any leftover rice from yesterday's dinner and extra ingredients and fry it all up for a filling meal.

Need a quick yet filling dish for your family? This seafood fried rice dish is a simple and filling comfort food that can be prepared easily within a short time!
